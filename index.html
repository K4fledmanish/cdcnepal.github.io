About CDC Nepal

Community Development Center (CDC) Nepal is a non-profit organization dedicated to improving the lives of people in underserved communities through sustainable development programs in health, education, and livelihood.

Our work is driven by a passionate team and the generous support of our partners and donors. We believe in creating lasting change through community-led initiatives.

What We Do

Health & Sanitation

Providing access to basic healthcare and promoting hygiene awareness.

Education For All

Ensuring every child has the opportunity to receive a quality education.

Sustainable Livelihood

Empowering communities with skills and resources for economic growth.
